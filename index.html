I'm Vince Tomlinson! A creative designer based in Manchester with over nine years of experience across presentation design, visual communication and digital production. My toolkit spans the full Adobe Creative Suite, PowerPoint, Keynote and beyond.

I spent eight years at Capgemini: first as a Collaborative Designer in the Accelerated Solutions Environment, facilitating complex problem-solving workshops using MG Taylor Methodology, then as a Creative Designer driving internal communications for HMRC, accumulating over 500 varied projects in graphic design, video editing and presentation design. Most recently I brought that experience to DRPG as a Presentation Designer and Operator for high-stakes live events and broadcasts.

Outside of design I have a passion for creative consulting and DEI training.

I firmly believe the best creative work comes from people who never stop being curious.
